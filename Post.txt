Advanced Financial Models 

Delving into advanced financial modeling!

I've learned about Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM), a model for asset prices that has the Markov property. Using Ito's Lemma, this leads to the famous Black-Scholes model for option pricing and their Greeks.

For more complex cases, Monte Carlo methods simulate thousands of price paths. Interestingly, for a standard European option, it converges with the Black-Scholes price!

We also covered the Vasicek model, a mean-reverting process for realistically simulating interest rates.

These tools provide powerful ways to value and hedge derivatives.
